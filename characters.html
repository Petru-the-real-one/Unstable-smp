Character desciption

ParrotX2

ParrotX2 is one of the main protagonists on Unstable SMP, known for clever strategies and bold plays.Recognised for intelligence, strategy, and a fearless approach to the game.ParrotX2 is player who can turn challenges into opportunities. ParrotX2 is a key protagonist and strategic player on the Unstable SMP, a high-stakes Minecraft server where players have only one life and PvP is always active. ParrotX2, also known simply as Parrot, is one of the main figures in the Unstable Universe, alongside SpokeIsHere, Wemmbu, and FlameFrags. He is recognized for his intelligence and strategic thinking rather than raw PvP skills, often using Minecraft mechanics to solve puzzles, gain advantages, and lead others. Parrot has held multiple leadership roles on the server, including king of the Unstable Universe, leader of District 13, and former head of the Warriors Council in Far Lands. He is known for his charitable nature, helping new players, and prioritizing teamwork over personal gain.

Spokeishere

SpokeIsHere, often called Spoke, is a chaotic and unpredictable protagonist (often mistaken as an antagonist) in the Unstable Universe SMP, known for his manipulative personality and complex relationships with other players.

Wemmbu

Wemmbu's role in the Unstable SMP is multifaceted, as he is not only a strategic player but also a key figure in the server's lore and narrative. His character is involved in various story arcs, including wars, faction politics, and high-stakes PvP battles. He is known for his tactical thinking and leadership in PvP. He also is known for competing with the player "FlameFrags" and doesn't quit easily.

TheobaldTheBird

TheobaldTheBird is a protagonist from the Unstable SMP known for being a supportive teammate of Parrot. He is characterized as a highly skilled and charismatic player, often focusing on PVP minecart battles. Theobald is known for his protective nature towards Parrot and is often seen as a comic relief character due to his tendency to lean into his humorous moments. Despite his playful demeanor, he can be prone to aggressive outbursts and has a short temper. Theobald's character adds a dynamic element to the Unstable SMP.

FlameFrags

FlameFrags is a central figure in the Unstable SMP, a staged Minecraft server featuring cinematic storytelling, PvP battles, and interactive gameplay. FlameFrags, also known as Flame, is one of the four main protagonists of the Unstable Universe series, alongside ParrotX2, SpokeIsHere, and Wemmbu. Originally a secondary antagonist in Season 1, Flame transitioned to a protagonist role in Season 2, providing his own perspective on the story. He is known for his combat skills, resilience, and strategic thinking, earning the title "The Immortal Demon." Flame is highly skilled in PvP, often taking on difficult challenges to defend his reputation, and he has a strong bond with his friend Lomedy, which plays a key role in his story arcs .

Eggchan

Eggchan, also known as Egg, is the deuteragonist in Wemmbu’s videos within the Unstable Universe, known for being calm, collected, and generally avoiding violence, preferring peaceful solutions whenever possible. He is highly observant, quickly noticing suspicious or sneaky behavior from other players, and is willing to risk himself to protect those he trusts. Despite being less skilled in gameplay than Wemmbu, Eggchan often serves as the strategic thinker in their duo.
